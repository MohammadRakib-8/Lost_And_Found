About Our Project

Lost and Found Management System

Project Overview

Our Lost and Found Management System is designed to help students and staff at AIUB report and recover lost or found items efficiently. Users can submit reports for lost or found belongings, and search or claim matched items. The goal is to provide a structured, user-friendly platform to make item recovery easier within the campus :)
